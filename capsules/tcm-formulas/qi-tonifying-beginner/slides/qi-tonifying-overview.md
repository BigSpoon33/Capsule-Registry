# Qi Tonifying Formulas: Overview
*CALE & NCCAOM Board Exam Preparation*

---

## Overview: Four Qi Tonifying Formulas

| Formula | Pattern | Chief Herb(s) | Key Feature |
|---------|---------|---------------|-------------|
| Si Jun Zi Tang | Spleen qi deficiency | Ren Shen | Foundation qi tonic; base for many formulas |
| Shen Ling Bai Zhu San | Spleen qi deficiency with internal dampness accumulation | Ren Shen, Bai Zhu | Si Jun Zi + dampness herbs; chronic diarrhea |
| Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang | Qi Sinking | Huang Qi, Ren Shen | Lifting herbs; organ prolapse |
| Sheng Mai San | Qi and yin deficiency with fluid loss and sweating | Ren Shen | Qi + Yin; sweating + thirst; red dry tongue |

---

## Si Jun Zi Tang (Four Gentlemen Decoction)
*Source: Chen & Chen p.556*

**Pattern:** Spleen qi deficiency

**Key Symptoms:**


**Tongue:** Pale, often with tooth marks, thin white coating  
**Pulse:** Weak, especially in the middle position (spleen/stomach)

**Chief Herb(s):** Ren Shen (Radix et Rhizoma Ginseng)

**Composition:**
- Ren Shen (Chief) — 9-15g
- Bai Zhu (Deputy) — 9-15g
- Fu Ling (Assistant) — 9-15g
- Gan Cao (Envoy) — 3-6g

**Actions:**
- Tonify qi
- Strengthen spleen
- Harmonize middle jiao

---

## Shen Ling Bai Zhu San (Ginseng, Poria, and Atractylodes Powder)
*Source: Chen & Chen p.574*

**Pattern:** Spleen qi deficiency with internal dampness accumulation

**Key Symptoms:**
- Chronic diarrhea
- abdominal distension
- edema
- fatigue
- loose stools

**Tongue:** Pale with thick white coating  
**Pulse:** Deep, weak, and slow

**Chief Herb(s):** Ren Shen (Radix et Rhizoma Ginseng), Bai Zhu (Rhizoma Atractylodis Macrocephalae)

**Composition:**
- Ren Shen (Chief) — 6-9g
- Bai Zhu (Chief) — 9-12g
- Fu Ling (Deputy) — 9-12g
- Gan Cao (Deputy) — 3-6g
- Shan Yao (Assistant) — 9-12g
- Lian Zi (Assistant) — 6-9g
- Bai Bian Dou (Assistant) — 9-12g
- Yi Yi Ren (Assistant) — 12-15g
- Sha Ren (Envoy) — 3-6g
- Jie Geng (Envoy) — 3-6g

**Actions:**
- Tonify spleen qi
- Resolve dampness
- Stop diarrhea
- Strengthen digestion

## Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang (Supplement the Middle and Augment the Qi Decoction)
*Source: Chen & Chen p.581*

**Pattern:** Spleen and stomach qi deficiency with qi sinking

**Key Symptoms:**
- Chronic Fatigue
- Chronic Diarrhea
- Dizziness
- Poor Appetite
- Prolapse

**Tongue:** Pale with thin white coating  
**Pulse:** Weak, especially in the middle position, may be thready

**Chief Herb(s):** Huang Qi (Radix Astragali), Ren Shen (Radix et Rhizoma Ginseng)

**Composition:**
- Huang Qi (Chief) — 15-30g
- Ren Shen (Chief) — 9-15g
- Bai Zhu (Deputy) — 9-15g
- Gan Cao (Deputy) — 6-9g
- Dang Gui (Assistant) — 6-12g
- Chen Pi (Assistant) — 6-9g
- Sheng Ma (Envoy) — 3-6g
- Chai Hu (Envoy) — 3-6g

**Actions:**
- Tonify middle jiao qi
- Raise yang qi
- Lift the clear

## Sheng Mai San (Generate the Pulse Powder)
*Source: Chen & Chen p.593*

**Pattern:** Qi and yin deficiency with fluid loss and sweating

**Key Symptoms:**
- Profuse Sweating
- Dry Mouth
- Fatigue
- Palpitations
- Restlessness

**Tongue:** Red and dry with little coating  
**Pulse:** Rapid and weak, or thready and rapid

**Chief Herb(s):** Ren Shen (Radix et Rhizoma Ginseng)

**Composition:**
- Ren Shen (Chief) — 6-9g
- Mai Men Dong (Deputy) — 9-15g
- Wu Wei Zi (Assistant) — 6-9g

**Actions:**
- Tonify qi
- Nourish yin
- Generate fluids
- Astringe sweat

## Key Differentiators

**Tongue:**
- Pale, thin white coating → Si Jun Zi Tang or Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
- Pale, thick white coating → Shen Ling Bai Zhu San (dampness)
- Red, dry, little coating → Sheng Mai San (yin deficiency)

**Key Symptom:**
- Pure fatigue, poor appetite → Si Jun Zi Tang
- Chronic diarrhea + dampness signs → Shen Ling Bai Zhu San
- Organ prolapse → Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
- Profuse sweating + thirst → Sheng Mai San

**Number of Herbs:**
- Si Jun Zi Tang: 4 herbs
- Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang: 8 herbs
- Shen Ling Bai Zhu San: 10 herbs
- Sheng Mai San: 3 herbs

## Exam Mnemonics

**Si Jun Zi Tang** — "Four Gentlemen": Ren Shen, Bai Zhu, Fu Ling, Gan Cao

**Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang** — "LIFT the center"
- **L**ift (Sheng Ma, Chai Hu)
- **I**mmunity (Huang Qi, largest dose)
- **F**uel digestion (Ren Shen, Bai Zhu)
- **T**onify blood (Dang Gui)

**Sheng Mai San** — "Generate Pulse (3 herbs)"
- Ren Shen: Qi
- Mai Men Dong: Yin + Fluids
- Wu Wei Zi: Astringe + Stop sweating

**Shen Ling Bai Zhu San** — "Si Jun + Dampness"
- Add: Shan Yao, Lian Zi, Bai Bian Dou, Yi Yi Ren (strengthen/dry)
- Add: Sha Ren (move qi), Jie Geng (raise qi mechanism)
