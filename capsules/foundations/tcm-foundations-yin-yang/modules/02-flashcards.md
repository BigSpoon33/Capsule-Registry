## What does Yin & Yang Theory in TCM primarily explain?
?
The Yin & Yang Theory explains the dynamic balance and interaction between two opposing yet complementary forces, Yin and Yang, which are integral to understanding the nature, location, and treatment of patterns of disharmony in the body.

---

## How is the Eight Principles Deficiency pattern classified?
?
The Eight Principles Deficiency pattern is classified under the Eight Principles with a Subtype of Deficiency.

---

## What are the key aspects of Eight Principles Deficiency?
?
Eight Principles Deficiency is characterized by a deficiency of either Yin, Yang, or both, leading to a range of clinical manifestations, with a primary nature of Deficiency, a varying nature of Hot/Cold (Cold or Heat), and an Interior location.

---

## What are the essential symptoms of Eight Principles Deficiency?
?
The essential symptoms of Eight Principles Deficiency include fatigue, weakness, pale complexion, shortness of breath, spontaneous sweating, loose stools, and poor appetite.

---

## What are the complete symptom manifestations of Eight Principles Deficiency?
?
The complete symptom manifestations of Eight Principles Deficiency include general fatigue and exhaustion, weakness, pale or sallow complexion, dizziness and lightheadedness, poor concentration and memory, shortness of breath, spontaneous sweating, loose stools or diarrhea, poor appetite and indigestion, cold limbs (if Yang deficiency), night sweats (if Yin deficiency), dry skin and hair.

---

## What does Yin represent in Yin & Yang Theory?
?
Yin in Yin & Yang Theory represents the cool, passive, and nourishing aspects.

---

## What does Yang represent in Yin & Yang Theory?
?
Yang in Yin & Yang Theory represents the warm, active, and transforming aspects.

---

## How is the pattern of Eight Principles Deficiency classified in terms of Interior/Exterior?
?
The pattern of Eight Principles Deficiency is always Interior, indicating a deeper level of deficiency.

---

## What are the clinical manifestations of Yin deficiency in Eight Principles Deficiency?
?
Yin deficiency in Eight Principles Deficiency can manifest as night sweats, dry skin and hair, and a general sense of internal heat.

---

## What are the clinical manifestations of Yang deficiency in Eight Principles Deficiency?
?
Yang deficiency in Eight Principles Deficiency can manifest as cold limbs, general fatigue, and a general sense of internal cold.
